Category 9: Skewverts

This category contains the honeycombs whose vertex figures are skewed wedges (dyad || dyad || dyad). This includes demicubic members of chonnic sphenovert regiments whose vertex figures have scalene triangular prism symmetry. There are two regiments of 15 plus four members from the srich and wavicoca regiments.

Srich regiment (half symmetrics)

These are the members of the srich regiment (category 4) with demicubic symmetry.

225. Skivdacdich - Small skewverted dicubatidicubatic honeycomb. Cells are sircoes, soccoes, ohoes, and gircoes. Symbol is x3x3/2o4/3x4*a3*c.

226. Skivticach - Small skewverted tricubaticubic honeycomb. Cells are srohs, choes, cubes, and gircoes.

227. Skivtica - Small skewverted tricubatiapeiratic honeycomb. Cells are srohs, choes, tosquats, and gircoes.

228. Skiv Dactica - Small skewverted dicubatitricubatiapeiratic honeycomb. Cells are soccoes, sircoes, ohoes, cubes, tosquats, and gircoes.
