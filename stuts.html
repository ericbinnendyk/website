Category 11: Stut Cadoca regiment

The stut cadoca regiment is the ultimate Wythoffian honeycomb regiment with 79 members, including 6 Wythoffians. The vertex figures are facetings of a triangular cupola with a regular hexagon as the base. This regiment has octet symmetry. This regiment is analogous to the stut phiddix and getit xethi regiments in Jonathan Bowers' polychoron list.

Conjugates

The following honeycombs are self conjugate: Any whose name starts with T.

The following form conjugate pairs: Those whose names start with "stut" or "getit". To find the conjugate, change "stut" at the beginning to "getit" or vice versa.
