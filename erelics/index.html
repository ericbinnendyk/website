Erelics

This is a page for works I created when I was younger which I think should be preserved. The name "Erelics" is inspired by Cary Huang's "Carelics" page.

  • The Soups Have What They Have, a song parody about searching patterns in Conway's Game of Life.
  • Non-notable Properties of Specific Numbers, made by me in December 2014. A parody of Robert Munafo's webpage "Notable Properties of Specific Numbers"
  • A 4th power poem about my life in 2012, where every line follows the digits of a 4th power up to 50^4
  • All the numbers from 1 to 100, a song based on Yakko's World
