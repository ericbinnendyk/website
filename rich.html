Category 3: Rich regiment

Rich is the rectified cubic honeycomb. The rich regiment contains 49 members with four symmetries: chonnic (o4o3x4o), octettic with one edge type (o3x4o *b3o), octettic with two edge types (x3o4o *b3x), and cyclotettic (o3x3o3x3*a). Their vertex figures are facetings of square prisms. There's also a compound of batches. The latter two symmetries were previously put in separate categories called "square podiumverts" and "antifrustumverts" but I decided to merge them into this category.

Rich regiment chonnics

These ones have verfs with full square prism symmetry.

22. Rich - Rectified cubic honeycomb. Cells are octs and coes. Symbol is o4o3x4o, o3x4o *b3o, x3o4o *b3x, or o3x3o3x3*a. Its verf is a square prism. This is also the rectified tetrahedral-octahedral honeycomb.

23. Doha - Disoctahemiapeiratic honeycomb. Cells are octs and thas. Symbol is o4o3x~x3/2*b.

24. Coha - Cuboctahedral hemiapeiratic honeycomb. Cells are coes and thas.

25. Hohah - Hemioctahedral hemiapeiratic honeycomb. Cells are choes and hohas.

26. Ohoha - Octahedral hemioctahedral hemiapeiratic honeycomb. Cells are octs, ohoes, and hohas.

27. Ohohath - Octahedral hemioctahedral hemitrihexagonal honeycomb. Cells are octs, choes, and thats.

28. Hohath - Hemioctahedral hemitrihexagonal honeycomb. Cells are ohoes and thats.
